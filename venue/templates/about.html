OUR STORY

A Venue Built for Your Memories

EVORIA Events is a premium venue located in the heart of Theni, Tamil Nadu. We were founded with one simple belief — every celebration deserves a grand, beautiful and stress-free space.

Whether it's an intimate birthday party or a grand marriage function, we provide a fully equipped venue with everything you need under one roof — spacious halls, dedicated parking, and optional catering service.

Our team is passionate about making your special day truly unforgettable. We handle the space — you focus on the celebration! 🎉
